## Major architectural lesson from this session (R220-R223)

Built `llgan/ird_diag.py` as a 2DIO-inspired (NOT 2DIO-cloned) per-stream IRD histogram diagnostic. Discovered that `fine_edges = [1, ..., 5669]` clipped the rank PMF — `np.histogram` silently dropped 15% of real alibaba's training signal during fit. R211/R213/R217 architectural sweeps couldn't move the floor because they were sampling from a clipped distribution.

R220 fix: extended `FINE_EDGES_R180` from 29 bins (max 5669) to 43 bins (max 251236) using log-spaced extension. Fix is **corpus-conditional**:
- Alibaba: helps marginally (+5%) and 4× tighter seed stability
- Tencent: HURTS (-17%) — deep tail too small to benefit, breaks fine-tuned R206 recipe
- CloudPhysics: HUGE WIN (+33%) — scan-like profile has heavy deep tail

Keys discovered along the way:
- Stack-cap (`max_stack_depth=8192`) was NOT the bottleneck (bumping to 524288 produced bit-identical output). The PMF binning was. Diagnose first, fix second.
- Per-corpus architecture choice is required: don't seek one architecture that wins all 3.
